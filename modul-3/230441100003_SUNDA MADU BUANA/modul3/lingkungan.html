Reuse

Reuse refers to the practice of using items, materials, or products again for the same or different purpose without significant alteration or processing. In the context of environmental sustainability, reuse is an important concept because it helps reduce waste generation and conserves resources.

Reduce

In the context of environmental sustainability, "reduce" refers to the practice of minimizing waste and consumption to lessen the environmental impact of human activities. It is one of the key principles in the "reduce, reuse, recycle" hierarchy, which emphasizes prioritizing actions to prevent waste generation in the first place.

Recycle

Recycling is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects. It is an essential component of waste management and environmental conservation efforts. Instead of disposing of used materials as waste, recycling allows these materials to be transformed into new products, reducing the consumption of raw materials, energy usage, and pollution associated with traditional manufacturing processes.
